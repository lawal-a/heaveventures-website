Why We Exist

The problems
we were built to solve

Africa's Biggest Institutions Are Being Disrupted

Banks, telcos, insurers and energy companies face startup disruption from every direction — yet most have no structured way to engage, co-build, or invest in the solutions threatening them.

Innovation Programmes Die Without Infrastructure

Most 'innovation' in Africa stops at the hackathon. Without programme continuity, hub management, and commercial pathways, good ideas never become real businesses.

The Silicon Valley Model Does Not Fit Africa's Tropical Savannah

Western frameworks don't map onto African market realities — informal networks, infrastructure gaps, policy constraints, and trust dynamics require a native approach.

How We Implement Innovation Projects

Our Approach

01

Diagnose

Map the institution's innovation maturity, strategic gaps, and ecosystem connectivity. Define what Corporate-Startup Fit looks like for this client.

02

Design

Architect the programme — open innovation model, pipeline structure, governance framework, and metrics. We don't template; we design.

03

Build

Stand up the infrastructure — digital platforms and sandboxes, physical hubs, programme workflows, and AI tooling. Execution with zero outsourcing opacity.

04

Run

Run the programme end-to-end: cohort intake, mentor networks, corporate matchmaking, investor connections, and continuous reporting.

05

Transfer

Transfer capability. Train client teams. Leave behind a self-sustaining innovation operation, not a dependency on Heave.
